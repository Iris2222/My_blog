Drawing acrylics

Decide what colors you need.

Choose 8–10 colors of artistic acrylic paints. If you are just starting to paint, 8–10 colors will be enough. Choose one paint tube of basic colors (blue, yellow and red) and 5–7 additional colors that you like to draw. For example: black; purple or pink; brown; green; white.


Select brushes.

Buy 5–8 art brushes for painting in various styles. Below is a list of the most common types of brushes for acrylic:
round brushes (for drawing lines and parts);
flat brushes (for creating large greasy strokes and painting large areas);
fan brushes (for mixing paints and blurring borders);
short cropped brushes (for close work with the canvas and creating clear thick strokes);
flat slanted brushes (for painting the corners and drawing small parts).


Get the basis for drawing acrylics.

As a simple base option, choose a primed canvas on a frame. If you are a novice artist, canvas will be the best material for you as a base. The canvas can be made of cotton or linen and sold in various forms, for example, on a stretcher and without it.



What to draw?

The most difficult question that stops and scares most emerging artists. You can spend a few hours or even days puzzling over inventing a brilliant idea for a drawing, but you can’t find anything. However, in doing so, you are not only imitating activities. Your skills and skills do not grow, but remain at the same level.


If you can not come up with an original idea - do not stand still! Take a look around. Put a still life of what is at hand.


Go for a walk and pick a couple of flowering branches or flowers. During the walk, find a beautiful stone and try to convey its texture.



First draw some abstract spot, and then, applying fantasy, try to see something special in it, add details. Thus, without thinking about what to draw, you will draw, maybe not the most original things, but they will help you to put your hand, feel the brush and paints, learn to work with light, shadow and contrasts. Draw, draw more, and ingenious creative thoughts will come with time! Good luck in your work and unlimited inspiration!
